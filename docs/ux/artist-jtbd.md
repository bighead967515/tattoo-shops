# JTBD Analysis — Artist

## Persona
- **Who**: Independent tattoo artist, 3–15 years experience
- **Device**: Mobile-first (DMs + photos), desktop for admin work
- **Tech level**: Comfortable with Instagram/TikTok, wary of complex SaaS dashboards
- **Context**: Running a one-person business, heavily reliant on DMs + word of mouth

## Job Statement
When I'm trying to fill my calendar with the right clients, I want a professional
online presence that showcases my style without managing a website, so I can attract
people who already want exactly what I do — without wasting hours on tire-kickers.

## Current Solution & Pain Points
- **Current**: Instagram + Google Business Profile + DMs
- Pain 1: Instagram shows ALL posts, not curated portfolio by style
- Pain 2: DM inquiries have no structure — "how much?" with zero context
- Pain 3: No-shows and ghosting waste time; no deposit mechanism
- Pain 4: License/credentials not visible → clients don't know they're legitimate
- Pain 5: Free artists can't see client requests → invisible opportunity

## Success Criteria
- Books ≥1 new client/month through the platform within 60 days of signup
- Spends <15 min/week managing their Ink Connect presence
